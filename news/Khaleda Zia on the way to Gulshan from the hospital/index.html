Khaleda Zia on the way to Gulshan from the hospital

by omar - 2020/03/25 76 views 0 Comment

Shamim Iskandar, his younger brother, is driving the car he is riding. Along with other members of his family. Besides, top leaders of the party, including BNP secretary general Mirza Fakhrul Islam Alamgir.

A large contingent of party leaders are also going with the car. At this time they used to give slogans.

On the occasion of the release of Khaleda Zia, the activists gathered at Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujib Medical University (BSMMU) hospital from Wednesday morning. Their enthusiasm for watching the patriarchs was eye-catching. They gather, ignoring the possibility of coronavirus infection.

Earlier, at an emergency press conference at his residence in Gulshan on Tuesday afternoon, law minister Anisul Haque said that the decision was taken to release BNP chairperson Begum Khaleda Zia under the orders of Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina.

 

She said Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina has ordered Khaleda Zia to be released conditionally due to humanitarian considerations. A recommendation has already been made in the Ministry of Home Affairs.

The law minister said Begum Zia's sentence will be suspended and released on two terms in the legal process. According to the Criminal Procedure Code-1 and Sub-section-3, it has been decided to suspend Begum Khaleda Zia's sentence for the next six months.

However, he will not be able to go abroad during this period. All kinds of medical services have to be received from within the country. He will be released at any time if the Home Ministry accepts the recommendation, added Anisul Haque.

The minister said that all the imprisonment of Khaleda Zia will be suspended for the next six months. It will be decided later on whether the period will be extended or not. Now he cannot go out of the country and get treatment from home.

However, Attorney General Mahbubey Alam warned on Wednesday that Khaleda's release could be canceled if conditions were broken.
